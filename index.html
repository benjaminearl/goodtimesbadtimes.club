Sporadic Hibernation Good Times Bad Times takes a break every now and then depending on the energies of the people and the space it's surrounded by. Don't worry, we'll be back at some point to provide more whimsical commentary and intimate space for you and your friends. Keep an eye on our Instagram and, in the meantime, check out our archived shows.

What did you have for breakfast this morning? How's your day looking? Whats the meaning of life? At Good Times Bad Times we want to talk about the most menial of subjects and how we live today. Together Jack, Kirsten and Ben, explore how to spend time in this precarious world and reflect on what we can do to enjoy the Good Times and make it through the Bad Times.


Good Times Bad Times is a community radio station at Extra Practice.
